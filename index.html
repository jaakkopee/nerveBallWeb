nerveBall

Welcome to nerveBall test site. Play at your own risk, the game is nerve wrecking and may cause anxiety. This site gathers no data, uses no cookies and has no ads. nerveBall is free software, licensed under GPL v3.

Click to split the ball and gain points. The smaller the ball, the more points you get. The faster the ball, the more points you get. Every 93 seconds The Big Ball appears and you lose points and time. The smallest balls do not split, but disappear and you gain play time. The game ends when you run out of time or all balls are gone.
